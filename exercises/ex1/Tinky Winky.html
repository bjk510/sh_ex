Tinky Winky

Not only is Tinky Winky the biggest Teletubby. He is also very kind and gentle. His favourite thing is his red bag. Tinky Winky loves walking, marching, dancing and falling over. He has a special song which he loves to sing to himself, 'Pinkle winkle, Tinky Winky, pinkle winkle, Tinky Winky'. Tinky Winky loves very big hugs.
He often hangs out with Poo .

His character has caused controversy due to allegations that his character's behaviour, bag, and body colour have homosexual qualities.
